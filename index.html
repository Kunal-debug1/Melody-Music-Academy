Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior music knowledge to join?

No! We welcome complete beginners. All you need is the passion to learn music.

What age groups do you accept?

We accept students of all ages - from children (6+ years) to adults. Music has no age limit!

Do I need to bring my own instrument?

For classes at the academy, instruments are provided. For home practice, we can guide you on purchasing.

What are the class timings?

Morning (9 AM - 12 PM), Evening (4 PM - 8 PM), and Weekend batches available.

Is there a free demo class?

Yes! We offer a completely FREE demo class. Contact us to book your session.
